Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored in the cloud?
No. All data (workouts, PRs, settings) is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.
What happens if I delete the app?
All local data is permanently erased. Use the Export & Backup feature (Pro) to save a JSON backup before uninstalling.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone → Apple ID → Subscriptions → MyWodScore → Cancel. You keep Pro access until the end of the billing period.
I was charged but Pro isn't active.
Go to My PRs or Settings → tap "Restore purchase". If the issue persists, email us with your Apple receipt and we'll sort it out.
Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Data is stored locally per device. Use the Backup (Pro) to export from one device and restore on another.
Which timer modes are free?
AMRAP, For Time, EMOM and Tabata are free. Mix Timer requires Pro.
The app crashed — what should I do?
Force-close the app and reopen. If it keeps crashing, email us with your device model and iOS version.
